Welcome to Music-Note-Count-o-Matic!
Three easy online steps to add note counts to your MusicXML or Guitar Pro score.
Upload your MusicXML or Guitar Pro file by clicking here or dragging it into this area.
The file will not be sent to any internet server. All processing is done within your browser and only on your computer. This service respects your privacy!
Click here in case you just want to try this service and load an example MusicXML score.
Choose the tracks you want to add note counts to.
All available tracks are selected by default, but maybe you want to refine the selection. Note counts are only added to the selected tracks.
Preview the processed file and download it.
You will get a preview of the first twelve measures of the whole score.
Made for other musicians with love by Mirko Sertic. Please report issues at Github.
